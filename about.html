about me.


Hello! I’m Shivam, a high schooler based in Edison, NJ. I study at the Middlesex County Academy for Science, Mathematics and Engineering Technologies.

I’m a passionate 17-year-old computer science enthusiast and an emerging social entrepreneur, with advanced knowledge in Python, artificial intelligence, machine/deep learning, BCIs, and cybersecurity. My goal is to pursue degrees in management and technology to better the lives of billions of people with disabilities around the world with assistive technology.

As an entrepreneur and philanthropist, I've founded two successful non-profits organizations, Speak2Lead and STEMninjas which have made a great impact in the education sector, a basic human need. STEMninjas provides academic assistance in STEM subjects to 600+ students in 5 continents and Speak2Lead teaches public speaking skills to youth across the US. Through these initiatives, I've cumulatively raised $10,000+ which has helped me fully fund 5 schools in India to teach 1,450+ underprivileged kids.

  • OpenCV
  • React
  • HTML & CSS
